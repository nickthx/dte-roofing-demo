### 2. Best Roofing Materials for Ohio Weather

**Location:** After "Our Replacement Process" and before "What's Included"
**Word Count:** ~950 words

**Content Structure:**
- Introduction to Central Ohio climate challenges (hot summers, cold winters, storms)
- Three detailed material analyses:

#### Asphalt Shingles (3-Tab)
- Description of economical option
- **Pros for Ohio Climate:**
  - Most affordable
  - Readily available
  - Adequate performance
  - Lightweight
- **Cons for Ohio Climate:**
  - Shorter lifespan (15-20 years)
  - Less wind resistance
  - Algae susceptibility
  - Lower hail resistance
  - Basic appearance

#### Architectural Shingles ⭐ Recommended
- Description of most popular Columbus choice
- **Pros for Ohio Climate:**
  - Extended lifespan (25-35 years)
  - Superior wind resistance (110+ mph)
  - Better impact resistance
  - Enhanced thermal performance
  - Algae-resistant options
  - Attractive appearance
- **Cons for Ohio Climate:**
  - Moderate price increase (20-40%)
  - Slightly heavier weight
  - Still vulnerable to extreme hail
- **Recommendation note** for Columbus homeowners

#### Metal Roofing
- Description of premium, long-term investment
- **Pros for Ohio Climate:**
  - Exceptional lifespan (40-70 years)
  - Superior extreme weather performance
  - Excellent hail resistance
  - Highly wind resistant (140 mph)
  - Reflective/energy efficient
  - Snow shedding capability
  - Fire resistant/eco-friendly
- **Cons for Ohio Climate:**
  - Higher upfront cost (2-3x shingles)
  - Potential noise in rain/hail
  - Requires specialized installation
  - Limited color options
  - May dent in severe hail
- **Best suited for** long-term homeowners
