### 2. Repair vs. Replacement Decision Guide (~350 words)

**Location:** Within problemPromise section

**When Repairs Are Sufficient:**
- Roof under 15 years old with localized damage
- Few damaged/missing shingles
- Identifiable, small leak sources
- Limited storm damage zones
- Sound deck structure
- Budget considerations

**Warning Signs for Replacement:**
- Roof over 20 years old with multiple problems
- Widespread shingle deterioration (curling, cupping, granule loss)
- Multiple leak locations
- Visible daylight through roof boards
- Sagging sections/structural issues
- Failed previous repairs
- Increased energy bills

**Cost-Benefit Analysis:**
- Central Ohio repair cost ranges ($500-$1,500 minor, $2,000-$4,000 major)
- 30% rule for repair vs. replacement decision
- Long-term financial considerations
- Honest assessment commitment

**Service Reference:** Professional roof inspection for comprehensive evaluation
